Description

Chocolate milk is a creamy, sweet beverage made by mixing milk with cocoa powder and sugar. Itâ€™s a comforting treat that balances the richness of chocolate with the smoothness of milk, offering a deliciously indulgent yet refreshing drink.

Ingredients

  • Raw milk
  • Raw honey
  • Cacao powder

Steps

  1. Warm a cup of milk (heat gently on the stove or in the microwave).
  2. Mix in 1-2 tablespoons of cacao powder (depending on your desired chocolate intensity).
  3. Add 1-2 teaspoons of honey (or to taste) and stir until dissolved.
  4. Whisk well to combine all the ingredients and ensure the cacao powder is fully dissolved.
  5. Enjoy warm, or chill it in the fridge for a cool treat!
