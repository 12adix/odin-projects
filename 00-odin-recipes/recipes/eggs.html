eggs

Description

Eggs are a versatile and nutritious food, known for their rich, tender yolks and firm, protein-packed whites. They can be cooked in a variety of ways—boiled, scrambled, fried, or poached—and are often used as a key ingredient in baking and cooking for their binding and leavening properties.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lard/tallow/butter/olive oil

Steps

  1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a bowl and whisk them with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and add a small pat of butter or oil.
  3. Pour the eggs into the skillet and let them sit for a few seconds to start setting.
  4. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing them from the edges to the center.
  5. Continue cooking until the eggs are soft and just set, then remove from heat and serve immediately.
